AN ACT

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 13-4424, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE13-4424.Impact statement; presentence report
A.In
preparing the presentence report, the victim may submit a written
impact statement or make an oral impact statement to the probation officer for
the officer's use in preparing a presentence report.
B.The probation officer shall consider the
economic, physical and psychological impact that the criminal offense has had
on the victim and the victim's immediate family pursuant to section 12253.
END_STATUTE
