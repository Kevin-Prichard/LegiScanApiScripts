(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 1, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-117, to read:
START_STATUTE15-117.Enrollment incentives; electronic devices; prohibition
A public school may not
offer to a child or a parent any electronic device as an incentive to enroll a
child at the school, regardless of whether the device was purchased with public
monies or monies from a private source. END_STATUTE
