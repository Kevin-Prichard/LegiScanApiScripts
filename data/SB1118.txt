Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.33,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Office of tourism; termination July 1, 2024
A.The
office of tourism terminates on July 1, 2024.
B.Title 41,
chapter 19 is repealed on January 1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 412955, subsection B, Arizona
Revised Statutes, the legislature continues the office of tourism so that the
office may:
1.Promote and develop tourism business and
planning.
2.Develop a campaign of information, advertising,
promotion, exhibition and publicity relating to tourism business, including the
recreational, scenic and historical attractions of this state and all
communities and regions of this state.
3.Disseminate this information to the public
through various state, national and international media.
Sec.4.Retroactivity 
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
