AN ACT

38852.01, Arizona Revised Statutes; relating to the public safety
personnel retirement system.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 38-852.01, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE38-852.01.Benefits not to be reduced by social security payments
From and after the
effective date of this section April 29, 1973, all benefits now or hereafter received pursuant to this
article or prior retirement systems shall not be reduced because of any payment
received as benefits under the federal old age and survivors insurance system. END_STATUTE
