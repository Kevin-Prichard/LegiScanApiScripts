Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 28, chapter 2, article 3,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 28-377, to read:
START_STATUTE28-377.National motor vehicle title information system; state and
local enforcement
notwithstanding
any other law, the department shall adopt rules to allow the department and
local authorities to enforce the National Motor Vehicle Title Information
System, 28 Code of Federal Regulations, chapter i, Part 25, Subpart B in effect
on January 1, 2016.END_STATUTE
