relating to dependent children.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 8, chapter 4, article 4,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 8-527.01, to read:
START_STATUTE8-527.01.Children in out-of-home care; transportation in taxi
The department, a child
welfare agency or a group foster home may not transport a child who is under
twelve years of age or a child who is noncommunicative in a taxi, unless the
child is accompanied by an adult other than the person driving the taxi. END_STATUTE
