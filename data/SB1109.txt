(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.16,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Arizona health facilities authority; termination July 1,
2024
A.The
Arizona health facilities authority and its governing board terminate on July
1, 2024.
B.Title 36,
chapter 4.2, article 1 is repealed on January 1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 412955, subsection B, the
legislature continues the Arizona health facilities authority and its governing
board to promote the construction, repair and improvement of health facilities
through its bonding authority.
Sec.4.Retroactivity
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
