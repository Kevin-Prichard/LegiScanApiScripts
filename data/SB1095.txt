41-3016.25, Arizona Revised Statutes; amending title 41, chapter 27, article 2,

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.25,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3020.01, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3020.01.Department of revenue; termination July 1, 2020
A.The
department of revenue terminates on July 1, 2020.
B.Title 42,
chapter 1, article 1 is repealed on January 1, 2021. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 41-2955, subsection B, Arizona Revised
Statutes, the legislature continues the department of revenue to efficiently
and fairly administer and collect the taxes levied by this state and to
determine the valuation of centrally assessed property in this state.
Sec.4.Retroactivity
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
