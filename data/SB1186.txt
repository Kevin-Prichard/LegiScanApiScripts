Section1.Section 35-513, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE35-513.Rebate setaside; authorization
The state treasurer or the treasurer of any political
subdivision may set aside such amounts as may be directed by the governing body
or that may in such
treasurer's discretion be sufficient to meet potential rebate requirements. END_STATUTE
