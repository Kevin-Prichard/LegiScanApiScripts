Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.10,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Prescott historical society of Arizona; termination July
1, 2024
A.The
Prescott historical society of Arizona terminates on July 1,2024.
B.Title 41,
chapter 4.1, article 2 is repealed on January 1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 41-2955, subsection B, Arizona Revised
Statutes, the legislature continues the Prescott historical society of Arizona
in order to purchase, receive, hold, lease and sell property for the benefit of
this state and the use of the society.
Sec.4.Retroactivity 
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1,2016.
