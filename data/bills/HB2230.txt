Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 7, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-720.03, to read:
START_STATUTE15-720.03.High schools; college accessibility awareness; college
admissions checklist
A.Each high
school shall establish a college accessibility awareness campaign to
communicate information to students enrolled at that school about college
admissions requirements and the financial aid application process.
B.Each
report card issued to each student in grades ten, eleven and twelve shall
include a checklist that indicates that student's progress toward completing
the admissions requirements for universities under the jurisdiction of the
Arizona board of regents. END_STATUTE
