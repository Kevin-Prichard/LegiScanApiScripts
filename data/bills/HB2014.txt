AN ACT

amending title 41,
Arizona Revised Statutes, by adding chapter 53; regarding electronic documents.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 41, Arizona Revised Statutes,
is amended by adding chapter 53, to read:
CHAPTER 53
ELECTRONIC DOCUMENTS
ARTICLE 1.GENERAL PROVISIONS
START_STATUTE41-5251.Electronic documents; definition
A.Notwithstanding
any other law, if a statute or rule requires a person to submit a report, audit
or application or any other documentation to any agency, the person may submit
the report, audit, application or other documentation to the agency
electronically.
b.Any
agency that requires a person to submit a report, audit or application or any
other documentation shall provide a method on the agency's website for the
person to submit the report, audit, application or other documentation
electronically.
C.For the
purposes of this section, "agency" has the same meaning prescribed in
section 412952. END_STATUTE
Sec.2.Effective date
This act is effective from and after June 30, 2018.
