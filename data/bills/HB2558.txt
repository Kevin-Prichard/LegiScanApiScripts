Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 12-569, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE12-569.Nonadmissibility of certain types of evidence relating to
professional liability insurance
During the trial of a medical malpractice action against a
licensed health care provider or during the course of any hearing or review conducted pursuant to section 12567,
evidence that any party or that any witness testifying in such trial, hearing, or review proceeding has
been or is covered by a professional liability insurance policy issued by a
health care insurer established pursuant to title 20, chapter 7, article 2 or
that such party or witness has a financial interest in the operation of such a
health care insurer arising as a result of the ownership of stock, a policy or
policies of insurance, notes, including contributed surplus notes, any other
evidence of indebtedness, or otherwise, shall not be received in evidence for
any purpose. END_STATUTE
