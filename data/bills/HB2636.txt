(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title
15, chapter 1, article 8, Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding
section 15-186, to read:
START_STATUTE15-186.New charter schools; location restriction
A.A charter school that is established after December
31, 2016 may not be located within five miles of a school that has been
assigned a letter grade of A or B pursuant to section 15241 within the
previous three years.
B.Subsection A of this section does not apply to any
charter school that was established before January 1, 2017. END_STATUTE
