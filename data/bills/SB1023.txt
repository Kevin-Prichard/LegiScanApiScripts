Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; Arizona arts endowment fund 
The sum of $20,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the Arizona arts endowment fund established by
section 41-986, Arizona Revised Statutes.
