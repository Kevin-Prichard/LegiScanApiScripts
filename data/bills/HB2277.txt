Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of health services; information
and referral service
The sum of $1,250,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of health services for an
information and referral service for health, community, human and governmental
services. The department may provide the information and referral service
directly or may contract with a private entity to provide the information and
referral service.
