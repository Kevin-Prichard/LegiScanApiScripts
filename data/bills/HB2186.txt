AN ACT

amending section
35118, Arizona Revised Statutes; relating to budget preparation.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 35-118, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE35-118.Transmission of budget report to legislature
The governor shall have the budget report prepared in such
number of copies as he the governor deems necessary,
and copies thereof of the budget report shall be
transmitted to the legislature not later than five days after the regular
session of the legislature convenes. END_STATUTE
