(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 17-337, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE17-337.Hunting and fishing licenses; armed forces members and
spouses
A member or the spouse
of a member of the armed forces of the United States who is on
active duty and stationed in this state for either permanent or temporary duty
may purchase a resident license permitting the taking of wildlife. END_STATUTE
