Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Subject
to the requirements of article IV, part 1, section 1, Constitution of Arizona,
title 16, chapter 6, article 2, Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding
section 16-956.01, to read:
START_STATUTE16-956.01.Random audits; corporate, limited liability company and
labor organization independent expenditures; procedure
A.After each general election, the commission shall
conduct random audits from among those corporations, limited liability
companies or labor organizations that register pursuant to section 16914.02
and make independent expenditures in that election cycle. Random audits shall
be conducted in accordance with procedures established by the commission and
shall include the review of notifications, related documentation and other
financial records to determine whether the corporation, limited liability
company or labor organization is in compliance with section 16914.02,
subsection K.
B.The Commission may hire an independent accounting firm
to conduct the random audits. The selection of any corporation, limited
liability company or labor organization for audit shall be determined by random
lot at a commission meeting. The audit is confidential unless a finding is
made that a violation has occurred, in which case the commission shall notify
the appropriate filing officer of the alleged violation. END_STATUTE
Sec.2.Requirements for enactment; three-fourths vote
Pursuant to article IV, part 1, section 1, Constitution of
Arizona, section 16-956.01, Arizona Revised Statutes, as added by this act, is
effective only on the affirmative vote of at least three-fourths of the members
of each house of the legislature.
