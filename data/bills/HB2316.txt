Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; water infrastructure finance authority of
Arizona; water supply and infrastructure projects; Navajo nation
The sum of $3,425,370 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the water infrastructure finance authority of
Arizona for distribution to the Navajo nation for the purpose of planning and
developing the water supply and infrastructure projects necessary to support
community growth and economic development on tribal land.
