AN ACT

requiring the
department of education to submit a report and make recommendations CONCERNING
educator recruitment and retention.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Department of education; educator recruitment and
retention; report; recommendations
Not later than January 31, 2017, the department of education
shall submit a report and present recommendations to a joint meeting of the
education committees of the senate and the house of representatives concerning
educator recruitment and retention.
