Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.06,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Department of administration; termination July
1,2024
A.The
department of administration terminates on July 1, 2024.
B.Title 41,
chapter 4, articles 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 7 and chapter 32 are repealed on January
1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 41-2955, subsection B, Arizona Revised
Statutes, the legislature continues the department of administration in order
to provide services to state government in the following areas:
1.Automation.
2.Finance.
3.Personnel administration.
4.Public buildings maintenance.
5.Surplus property.
6.Risk management.
Sec.4.Retroactivity 
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1,2016.
