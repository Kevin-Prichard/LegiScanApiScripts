A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

Proposing an
amendment to the constitution of Arizona; repealing article XXV, constitution
of arizona; relating to the right to work.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of
Arizona, the Senate concurring:
1.Article XXV,
Constitution of Arizona, is proposed to be repealed as follows if approved by
the voters and on proclamation of the Governor:
Article XXV,
Constitution of Arizona, relating to the right to work, is repealed.
2.The Secretary of
State shall submit this proposition to the voters at the next general election
as provided by article XXI, Constitution of Arizona.
