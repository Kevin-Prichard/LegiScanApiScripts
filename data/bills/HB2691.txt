AN ACT

providing for a

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Special audit; refugee resettlement process; delayed
repeal
A.The auditor general shall conduct a special audit
pursuant to section 41-1279.03, Arizona Revised Statutes, of the refugee
resettlement process in this state under the federal refugee resettlement
program (8 United States Code sections 1521 through 1525).
B.The audit shall review all of the following:
1.The number of refugees that have been resettled
in this state in the last thirtysix months pursuant to the refugee
resettlement program.
2.The amount of state monies that has been spent on
refugee resettlement pursuant to the refugee resettlement program.
3.The amount of federal monies that this state has
received pursuant to the refugee resettlement program.
C.On or before December 1, 2016, the auditor
general shall submit copies of the special audit to the governor, the president
of the senate and the speaker of the house of representatives and provide a
copy of this report to the secretary of state.
D.This section is repealed from and after September
30, 2017.
