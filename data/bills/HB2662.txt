Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 8-452, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE8-452.Director; appointment; qualifications; compensation
A.The governor shall appoint the director of the
department pursuant to section 38211.The director serves at
the pleasure of the governor.
B.At a minimum, the director shall have:
1.Administrative
experience in the protection of children from maltreatment and in family
support services.
2.Qualifications
and training that enable the director to manage the affairs of the department.
1.A
master's or doctoral degree in social work, public health, human services,
public administration or policy, law, clinical psychology or a closely related
field.
2.At least
twelve years of experience in the provision and supervision of child welfare,
public health or social services programs, including evidence of community
collaborations.
3.At least
two years of demonstrated successful experience in the management of a large
health or human services system in either the governmental or nonprofit sector
or a related sector and evidence of collaboration and effective partnerships
with the community.
C.The director is eligible to receive compensation
pursuant to section 38611. END_STATUTE
