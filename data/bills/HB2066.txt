Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 1, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-117, to read:
START_STATUTE15-117.Daily physical activity; policy; public meeting; exemption
A.On or
before January 1, 2017, each school district governing board and each charter
school governing body shall conduct a public meeting to consider adopting a
policy to provide daily physical activity to pupils in kindergarten programs
and grades one through five. The school district or charter school shall
notify each parent of a child who is enrolled in the school district or charter
school, as applicable, of any public meeting called pursuant to this subsection
and shall allow any parent to comment on the proposed policy at that public meeting.
The school district governing board or charter school governing body shall
discuss and vote on the adoption of the proposed daily physical activity policy
after receiving parent comments on the proposed policy.
B.If a
daily physical activity policy is adopted pursuant to subsection A of this
section:
1.The daily
physical activity requirements shall consist of structured or unstructured
physical activity inside or outside of the classroom.
2.Physical
education classes may be used to satisfy the requirements of this section.
3.Unstructured
recess time that occurs immediately before or after lunch periods does not
satisfy the requirements of this section.
C.School
districts and charter schools that do not provide instruction to pupils in
kindergarten programs or grades one through five are not required to conduct a
public meeting to consider adopting a daily physical activity policy pursuant
to subsection A of this section. END_STATUTE
