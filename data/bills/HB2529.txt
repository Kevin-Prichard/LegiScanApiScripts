Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 46, chapter 2, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 46-219, to read:
START_STATUTE46-219.Supplemental nutrition assistance; fresh food bonus
program; fund; program termination
A.The
department shall establish a fresh food bonus program for enrollees of the
supplemental nutrition assistance program that will provide matching monies for
an enrollee who purchases fresh fruits and vegetables at a farmers
market.The department may contract with a third party to administer
the fresh food bonus program. Subject to available monies, the fresh food
bonus program shall provide matching monies to supplemental nutrition
assistance program monies that are used to purchase fresh fruits and vegetables
at a farmers market.An enrollee in the supplemental nutrition
assistance program may receive a maximum of ten dollars per market per day in
matching monies.
B.The fresh
food bonus fund is established consisting of federal grants and private grants,
gifts and donations.The department shall administer the fund.
Monies in the fund are continuously appropriated. The fund shall be used for
the matching monies provided under the fresh food bonus program and any costs
associated with the fresh food bonus program, including the administration and
marketing of the fresh food bonus program.
C.The fresh
food bonus program established by this section ends on July 1, 2026 pursuant to
section 413102.END_STATUTE
