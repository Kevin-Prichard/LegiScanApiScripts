Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 28-4805, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE28-4805.Towing company; reimbursement
If a vehicle is abandoned pursuant to section 284802 on national forest, state park, bureau of land
management or state trust land and a fee is collected by the
department, the towing company that towed the abandoned vehicle off of the land, if still in business, is
entitled to receive one hundred dollars all towing fees from the fee
collected. END_STATUTE
