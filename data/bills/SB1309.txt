(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 40, chapter 2, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 40-211, to read:
START_STATUTE40-211.Public service corporations; electronic filings
Notwithstanding any
other law, the commission may accept electronically any document that a public
service corporation is required to submit pursuant to this chapter, any rules
adopted pursuant to this chapter or a commission order. END_STATUTE
