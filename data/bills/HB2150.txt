Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 12, chapter 6, article 12,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 12-720, to read:
START_STATUTE12-720.Car rental surcharge determination; no liability for
refund or damages 
If a court of competent
jurisdiction makes a final determination that the car rental surcharge that is
collected by the department of revenue pursuant to section 5839 is
unconstitutional, a car rental company that collected the surcharge from a car
rental customer is not liable in any civil action for a refund of the monies
that were collected by the rental car company before the law was declared
unconstitutional or for any damages that are a direct result of the final
determination that the surcharge is unconstitutional. END_STATUTE
