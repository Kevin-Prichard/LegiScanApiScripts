Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 44, chapter 11, Arizona
Revised Statutes, is amended by adding article 26, to read:
ARTICLE 26.HOTEL AND MOTEL EMPLOYEES
START_STATUTE44-1799.95.Employee guest room access; registered sex offenders
prohibition
Before a hotel or motel
owner or manager allows a hotel or motel employee to have access to the room of
a registered guest of the hotel or motel, the owner or manager must check the
internet sex offender website established pursuant to section 13-3827 and the
United States department of justice national sex offender public
website.If the employee appears on either website as a sex
offender, the employee may not have access to the room of a registered guest of
the hotel or motel. END_STATUTE
