AN ACT

amending Title
41, chapter 32, article 1, Arizona Revised Statutes, by adding section 41-3509;
relating to government information technology.

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 41, chapter 32, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3509, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3509.Computer systems; software; sale or lease; deposit
A.Notwithstanding
section 412752, a budget unit may produce software or computer systems
and provide the software or computer systems and associated services and
intellectual property to other budget units at no cost or offer the software or
computer systems and associated services and intellectual property for sale or
lease to other budget units or government agencies or private entities.
B.The
budget unit shall deposit, pursuant to sections 35146 and 35147,
sixty percent of the profit from the monies generated by the sale or lease of
the software or computer systems and associated services and intellectual
property pursuant to subsection A of this section in the state general fund,
and the remaining forty percent may be retained by the budget unit. END_STATUTE
