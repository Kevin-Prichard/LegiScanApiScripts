AN ACT

amending title

relating to school buses.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title
28, chapter 3, article 15, Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding
section 28-913, to read:
START_STATUTE28-913.School buses; lap belts required
Beginning January 1, 2017, each school bus that is purchased
for use in this state must be equipped with lap belts. END_STATUTE
