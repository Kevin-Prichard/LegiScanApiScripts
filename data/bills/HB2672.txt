(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 3, article 6,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-397, to read:
START_STATUTE15-397.School district buildings; notice to joint districts
If a school district
intends to sell or lease a vacant and unused building or portion of a building,
the school district shall give written notice to the governing board of each
joint technical education district in this state. Notwithstanding any other
law, a school district may not negotiate to sell or lease the property with any
potential buyer or lessee other than a joint district during the first ninety
days after giving notice pursuant to this section.END_STATUTE
