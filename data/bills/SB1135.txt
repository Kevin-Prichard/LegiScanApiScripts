AN ACT

amending title
28, chapter 3, article 15, Arizona Revised Statutes, by adding section 28-913;
relating to the use of handheld wireless communication devices while driving.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 28, chapter 3, article 15,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 28-913, to read:
START_STATUTE28-913.Handheld wireless communication device use while operating
public transit, livery vehicles, taxis, limousines, transportation network
vehicles or buses; prohibition; exception
A.A person may not use a handheld wireless
communication device to make or receive a telephone call, to engage in a telephone
conversation or to manually write, send or read a written message while
operating a vehicle for public transit, a livery vehicle, a taxi, a limousine,
a transportation network vehicle or a bus.
B.This
section does not apply to a person who dials the telephone number 911.
END_STATUTE
