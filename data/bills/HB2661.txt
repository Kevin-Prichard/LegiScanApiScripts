Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 35, chapter 3, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended by adding article 8, to read:
ARTICLE 8.PAYMENT OF PAYMENT DEFERRALS
START_STATUTE35-521.Appropriations; payment of payment deferrals
In addition to any
other monies appropriated, the following amounts are appropriated from the
state general fund in fiscal years 2017-2018 through 2026-2027 to the following
budget units for payment of payment deferrals made in prior fiscal years:
1.$93,072,770
to the department of education.
2.$7,476,040
in to Arizona state university  Tempe and downtown Phoenix campuses.
3.$575,020
to Arizona state university  East campus.
4.$1,006,480
to Arizona state university  West campus.
5.$3,049,480
to Northern Arizona university.
6.$6,215,310
to university of Arizona  main campus.
7.$1,677,670
to university of Arizona  health sciences center.
8.$2,100,000
to the department of economic security.
9.$1,100,000
to the department of child safety. END_STATUTE
Sec.2.Delayed repeal
Title 35, chapter 3, article 8, Arizona Revised Statutes, as
added by this act, is repealed from and after September 30, 2029.
