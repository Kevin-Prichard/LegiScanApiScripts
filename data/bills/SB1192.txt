Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; Arizona water protection fund
The sum of $1,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the Arizona water protection fund established
by section 45-2111, Arizona Revised Statutes.
