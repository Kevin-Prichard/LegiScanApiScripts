Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 20-112, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE20-112.Existing actions or violations
Repeal by this title of any law shall not affect or abate any
right accrued, action or proceeding commenced, or any unlawful act committed
under such law, prior to January 1, 1955, and punishment or deprivation of
license or authority as a consequence thereof as provided by such laws, but all
proceedings taken on or after January 1, 1955 with respect thereto shall
conform to the applicable provisions of this code title
insofar as possible.All such laws shall be deemed to
continue in force to the extent made necessary by this provision. END_STATUTE
