(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 41-328, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE41-328.Prohibited conduct; incomplete documents; signatures of
relatives
A.A notary public shall not perform a jurat on a
document that is incomplete. If a notary public is presented with a document
that the notary knows from experience to be incomplete or if the document on
its face is incomplete, the notary public shall refuse to perform the jurat.
B.A notary public is an impartial witness and shall
not notarize the notary's own signature or the signatures of any person who is
related to the notary by blood, marriage
or adoption.
C.Subject to section 41320, a notary public
shall not perform a notarization on a document if the notary is an officer of
any named party, if the notary is a party to the document or if the notary will
receive any direct material benefit from the transaction that is evidenced by
the notarized document that exceeds in value the fees prescribed pursuant to
section 41316. END_STATUTE
