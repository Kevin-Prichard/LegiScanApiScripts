Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of education; teacher mentoring
programs
A.The sum of $15,000,000 is appropriated from the
state general fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of education for
distribution to school districts and charter schools to fund teacher mentoring
programs. A program shall require a mentor teacher to work with a mentee
teacher on lesson planning, classroom management, time management and other
areas of professional development, as determined by the mentor teacher and the
principal or other administrative head of the school.
B.A school district or charter school that conducts
a teacher mentoring program as prescribed in subsection A of this section shall
report to the department of education the number of first-year, second-year and
third-year teachers being mentored in the program.Subject to the
appropriation made in subsection A of this section, the department shall
distribute to the school district or charter school the following amount:
1.$1,500 for each first-year teacher in the
program.
2.$1,000 for each second-year teacher in the
program.
3.$500 for each third-year teacher in the program.
C.A school district or charter school receiving
monies pursuant to this section shall use the monies to pay mentor teachers a
stipend in the following amounts:
1.$1,500 for mentoring a first-year teacher.
2.$1,000 for mentoring a second-year teacher.
3.$500 for mentoring a third-year teacher.
