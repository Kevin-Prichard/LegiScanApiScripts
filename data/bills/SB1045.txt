AN ACT

repealing section
413016.28, Arizona Revised Statutes; amending title 41, chapter 27,
article 2, Arizona Revised Statutes, by adding section 413024.09;
relating to the Arizona pioneers' home and state hospital for miners with
disabilities.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.28,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Arizona pioneers' home; state hospital for miners with
disabilities; termination July 1, 2024
A.The
Arizona pioneers' home and the state hospital for miners with disabilities
terminate on July 1, 2024.
B.Title 41,
chapter 5, articles 2 and 3 are repealed on January 1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 412955, subsection B, Arizona
Revised Statutes, the legislature continues the Arizona pioneers' home and the
state hospital for miners with disabilities to provide services to longterm
residents and miners of this state.
Sec.4.Retroactivity 
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
