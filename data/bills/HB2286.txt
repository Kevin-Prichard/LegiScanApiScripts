Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Supplemental appropriation; department of child safety;
foster care placement rates
In addition to the appropriation made by Laws 2015, chapter 8,
section 21, the sum of $3,200,000 is appropriated from the state general fund
in fiscal year 2015-2016 to the department of child safety to increase foster
care placement rates.
