Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 42-3009, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE42-3009.Exemption for articles and substances sold in interstate
commerce
The tax imposed by this chapter shall not be imposed upon on any article or substance that is sold in interstate commerce and that
this state is prohibited from taxing under the Constitution of the United
States.END_STATUTE
