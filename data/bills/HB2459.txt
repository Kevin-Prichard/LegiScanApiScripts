Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Supplemental appropriation; department of child safety;
attorneys
In addition to the appropriation made by Laws 2015, chapter 8,
section 21, the sum of $1,000,000 and 22 FTE positions are appropriated from
the state general fund in fiscal year 2015-2016 to the department of child
safety for additional protective services section attorneys and support staff.
Sec.2.Appropriation; department of child safety; attorneys
The sum of $8,028,000 and 85 FTE positions are appropriated
from the state general fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of child
safety for protective services section attorneys and support staff.
Sec.3.Appropriation; department of child safety; attorney
retention
The sum of $1,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of child safety to increase
protective services section attorney salaries and employee-related expenses for
the purpose of retaining protective services section attorneys.
