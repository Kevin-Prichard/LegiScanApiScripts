AN ACT

relating to court case records.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 12, chapter 1, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 12-119.06, to read:
START_STATUTE12-119.06.Publicly accessible and searchable case records; case
disposition 
If a court maintains
case information for a publicly accessible and searchable case record database
that is searchable by a person's name or a case number, the court shall
prominently post a clear and concise disposition for each listed case within
thirty days after the final disposition of the case, including whether the case
was dismissed, whether the person was found guilty or responsible or not guilty
or not responsible or pled no contest or whether any other final judgment was
rendered. END_STATUTE
