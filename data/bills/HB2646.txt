(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 41, chapter 1, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-108, to read:
START_STATUTE41-108.Arizona film commission; fund
A.The Arizona
film commission is established consisting of the following members:
1.One
member who is appointed by the governor and who shall serve as the state film
commissioner.
2.Two
public members appointed by a film production association in this state.
B.The
commission shall:
1.Promote
film and media production in this state.
2.Coordinate
with other agencies to assist the film and media production industry in all
categories of production.
C.The
Arizona film commission fund is established consisting of private grants,
gifts, contributions and devises to assist in carrying out the PURPOSES of this
section. The Commission shall administer the fund. Monies in the fund are
CONTINUOUSLY appropriated.
D.The
commission shall be completely self funded and may conduct fundraising
activities.
END_STATUTE
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3019.06, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3019.06.Arizona film commission; termination July 1, 2019
A.The
Arizona film commission terminates on July 1, 2019.
B.Section
41108 is repealed on January 1, 2020. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 412955, subsection E, Arizona
Revised Statutes, the legislature establishes the Arizona film commission to
promote film and media production in this state.
