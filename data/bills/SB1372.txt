Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of water resources; city of
Buckeye; salt cedar removal
A.The sum of $200,000 is appropriated from the
state general fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of water
resources for distribution to the city of Buckeye for payment of costs incurred
by the city of Buckeye for research and studies associated with acquiring
federal and state permits for the removal of salt cedar trees from the Gila
river.
B.The appropriation made in subsection A of this
section is exempt from the provisions of section 35-190, Arizona Revised
Statutes, relating to lapsing of appropriations.
