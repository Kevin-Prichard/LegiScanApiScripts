Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section
6-121, Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE6-121.Examination; supervision
All financial institutions and
enterprises shall be subject to examination and supervision by the department. If during the examination the department discovers that the
products and services that are offered by the financial institutions and
enterprises violate federal or state law, the department shall refer the
violation to the attorney general for enforcement. END_STATUTE
