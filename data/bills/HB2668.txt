Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 36-139, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE36-139.Fees
A.The department of health services may charge fees
for:
1.Rental of films and film strips filmstrips.
2.Laboratory tests.
B.The amount of the fees charged for such services
shall not exceed the cost to the department of rendering them, and all fees
shall be deposited, pursuant to sections 35146 and 35147, at
least once each month in the state general fund.No rental or
laboratory fee shall be charged to or collected from a county, city, town,
school, or school district or any
political subdivision thereof. END_STATUTE
