Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 12-101, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE12-101.Justices; number; term; election
The supreme court shall consist of five judges seven justices.The term of office
of each judge justice shall be six years and
shall take effect on the first Monday in January of the year in which it
begins.Judges Justices shall be elected at
the general election next preceding the expiration of a term of office.
