Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title
15, chapter 7, article 5, Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding
section 15-790.01, to read:
START_STATUTE15-790.01.Appropriation; statewide information technology education
and certification program; annual report; program termination
A.The sum of $1,000,000 is appropriated from the
state general fund each fiscal year to the department of education to establish
a statewide information technology education and certification program in
school districts and charter schools that provide instruction for pupils in
kindergarten programs and grades one through twelve.
B.The department shall contract with at least
one vendor to provide software licenses, courses, certification examinations,
curricula and materials that prepare pupils for information technology industry
certification.
C.Beginning October 15, 2017 and on or before
October 15 of each year thereafter, the department shall prepare and submit to
the governor, speaker of the house of representatives, president of the senate
and secretary of state a report that contains:
1.The number of contracts awarded pursuant to
this section.
2.A list of participating school districts,
charter schools and joint technical education districts.
3.Performance data regarding the information
technology education and certification program, including performance data
regarding pupils participating in the program.
D.The program established by this section ends
on July 1, 2026 pursuant to section 413102. END_STATUTE
