Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; internet crimes against children; exemption
A.The sum of $1,800,000 is appropriated from the
internet crimes against children enforcement fund established by section 41199,
Arizona Revised Statutes, in fiscal year 2015-2016 to the attorney general for
the purposes prescribed in section 41199, subsection B, Arizona Revised
Statutes.
B.The appropriation made in subsection A of this
section is exempt from the provisions of section 35-190, Arizona Revised
Statutes, relating to lapsing of appropriations.
Sec.2.Emergency
This act is an emergency measure that is necessary to preserve
the public peace, health or safety and is operative immediately as provided by
law.
