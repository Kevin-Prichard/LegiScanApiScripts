Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 15-1452, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE15-1452.Evaluation of vocational and technical education programs
A district board shall provide
for the evaluation of vocational and technological
technical education
programs once every five years. The assessment shall be conducted in
cooperation with and with assistance from business, industry and labor
representatives. The district board may conduct a selfevaluation. END_STATUTE
