(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 3, chapter 2, article 5,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 3-357, to read:
START_STATUTE3-357.Prohibited pesticides
It is unlawful for a person to sell or use any of the
following pesticides in this state:
1.Clothianidin.
2.Imidacloprid.
3.Thiamethoxam. END_STATUTE
