Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 15-1303, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE15-1303.School for the deaf and the blind as public corporation;
tax exemption
A.The school shall be a body corporate under the
name of "the Arizona state school
schools for the deaf and
the blind".
B.The corporation shall:
1.Have perpetual succession.
2.Have a corporate seal.
3.Be deemed a public corporation and its property
shall be exempt from all taxes and assessments.
C.The corporation may, in the corporate name:
1.Sue and be sued.
2.Contract and be contracted with.
3.Take and hold by purchase, gift or devise real
and personal property required for its use.
4.Convert property received by gift, purchase or
devise which that is not suitable for its
use into money or property which
that is suitable for its
use. END_STATUTE
