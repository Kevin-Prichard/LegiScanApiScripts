Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 42-5038, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE42-5038.Transaction and privilege taxes independent of other taxes
Taxes that are imposed
by this article shall be are in addition to all other
licenses and taxes that are
levied by law, whether as a condition precedent to engaging in any business
taxable under this article or for any other purpose.END_STATUTE
