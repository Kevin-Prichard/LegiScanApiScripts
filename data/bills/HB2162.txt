Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 13-2204, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE13-2204.Defrauding secured creditors; classification; definition
A.A person commits defrauding secured creditors if
the person knowingly destroys, removes, conceals, encumbers, converts, sells,
obtains, transfers, controls or otherwise deals with property subject to a
security interest with the intent to hinder or prevent the enforcement of that
interest.
C.B.Defrauding
secured creditors is a class 6 felony.
B.C.For the purposes
of this section, "control" has the same meaning as prescribed by section 131801. END_STATUTE
