Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of health services; screening;
severe combined immunodeficiency; exemption
A.The sum of $890,000 is appropriated from the
state general fund in each of fiscal years 2016-2017, 2017-2018 and 2018-2019
to the department of health services to provide screening of newborns for
severe combined immunodeficiency.
B.The appropriations made in subsection A of this
section are exempt from the provisions of section 35-190, Arizona Revised
Statutes, relating to lapsing of appropriations.
