Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Laws 2014, chapter 227, section 1 is
amended to read:
Section1.United States submarine memorial; procedures; delayed repeal
A.Pursuant to section 411363, subsection A,
Arizona Revised Statutes, this act authorizes the department of administration
to provide for the placement in Wesley Bolin plaza of a memorial dedicated to
the commemoration of United States submarine veterans.
B.The procedures prescribed in section 411363,
subsection B, Arizona Revised Statutes, apply to the establishment of the monument memorial, except that the memorial shall be completed and dedicated to
this state on or before September 1, 2020.
C.Public monies are not authorized for the costs of
the monument memorial.All
fund-raising and contracts for artistic design and construction of the memorial
are the sole responsibility of the proponents. This state shall not facilitate
fundraising or establish a state fund for the deposit of the monies. 
D.This section is repealed from and after September
30, 2017 2020.
