AN ACT

amending section
3-123, Arizona Revised Statutes; relating to cooperative agricultural extension
work.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 3-123, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE3-123.Agricultural extension work
Cooperative agricultural extension work shall consist of giving
practical demonstrations in agriculture and home economics, and imparting
information on those subjects through field demonstrations, publications and
otherwise. The work shall be carried on in such manner as is mutually agreed
upon by the Arizona board
of regents of the universities and state
college of Arizona and the county agricultural extension board of
each county provided for in this article. END_STATUTE
