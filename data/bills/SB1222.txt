Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 42-1008, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE42-1008.Employee preparation of tax returns for compensation
prohibited
An employee of the department of revenue shall not for
compensation prepare or assist in preparing any tax return required to be filed
with the federal, the
state or a local government.A violation of this section shall be
grounds for immediate discharge
dismissal. END_STATUTE
