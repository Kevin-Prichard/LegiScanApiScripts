Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Air quality state implementation plan; waiver; Arizona
approved fuels shortage; CARB gasoline
The director of the department of environmental quality shall
apply to the United States environmental protection agency for a waiver from
certain requirements of the state implementation plan for air quality regarding
the use of Arizona clean burning gasoline and Arizona reformulated blendstock
for oxygenate blending to allow for the use of California air resources board
phase 3 gasoline during times of shortage in the supplies of these approved
Arizona fuels. The implementation of the waiver may be conditioned on the
director of the department of environmental quality, after consultation with
the director of the Arizona department of agriculture and the associate
director of the weights and measures services division, issuing a finding of
shortage in the supplies of Arizona clean burning gasoline or Arizona
reformulated blendstock for oxygenate blending.
