Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of health services; grants; pediatric
neurological autoimmune disorders
The sum of $1,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of health services to provide
grants on a competitive basis pursuant to title 41, chapter 24, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, for the research, diagnosis and treatment of
pediatric neurological autoimmune disorders.The biomedical research
commission established by section 36-272, Arizona Revised Statutes, shall
evaluate grant applications and shall make award recommendations to the
director of the department of health services.
