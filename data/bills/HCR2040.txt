A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

proposing an
amendment to the constitution of Arizona; amending article IX, constitution of
arizona, by adding section 25; relating to public investments.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of
Arizona, the Senate concurring:
1.Article IX, Constitution of Arizona, is proposed
to be amended by adding section 25 as follows if approved by the voters and on
proclamation of the Governor:
START_STATUTE25.Prohibited investment of public monies
Section
25.A.This state, a public retirement system, a county,
a city, a town or any other political subdivision of this state may not
contract with or invest in any individual, partnership, corporation or other
legal entity that is invested in or doing business with the government of a
country that is designated by the United States department of state on or after
January 1, 2015 as a state sponsor of terrorism.
B.Any
contract or investment that is in effect on the effective date of this section
and that is in violation of subsection A of this section may not be renewed and
shall be cancelled or divested at the earliest prudent opportunity, except that
the contract or investment shall be cancelled or divested on or before December
31, 2018.
C.The
prohibition in subsection A of this section does not apply to any country that
is designated by the United States department of state on or after January 1,
2015 as a state sponsor of terrorism if both of the following occur:
1.The
United States department of state removes the country's designation as a state
sponsor of terrorism.
2.A
measure removing the prohibition in subsection A of this section in regard to
the country is passed by the legislature and approved by the governor as
prescribed in this constitution. END_STATUTE
2.The Secretary of State shall submit this
proposition to the voters at the next general election as provided by article
XXI, Constitution of Arizona.
