AN ACT

amending Title
41, chapter 6, article 1, Arizona Revised Statutes, by adding section
41-1002.01; relating to state administrative procedures.

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 41, chapter 6, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-1002.01, to read:
START_STATUTE41-1002.01.Educational programs; limits; prohibition; definition
A.An agency
may not limit enrollment in any school or educational program of an institution
of higher education.
B.For the
purposes of this section, "agency" has the same meaning prescribed in
section 411001 but does not include the Arizona board of regents or any
community college district board. END_STATUTE
Sec.2.Emergency
This act is an emergency measure that is necessary to preserve
the public peace, health or safety and is operative immediately as provided by
law.
