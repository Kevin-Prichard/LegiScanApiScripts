Be it resolved by the House of Representatives of the State of
Arizona, the Senate concurring:
1.Under the power of the referendum, as vested in
the Legislature, the following measure, relating to the citizens clean
elections act, is enacted to become valid as a law if approved by the voters
and on proclamation of the Governor:
AN ACT
repealing title 16,
chapter 6, article 2, Arizona Revised Statutes; relating to the citizens clean
elections act.
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Title 16, chapter 6, article 2, Arizona Revised Statutes, is
repealed.
Sec.2.Conforming legislation
The legislative council staff shall prepare proposed
legislation conforming the Arizona Revised Statutes to the provisions of this
act for consideration in the fifty-third legislature, first regular session.
2.The Secretary of State shall submit this
proposition to the voters at the next general election as provided by article
IV, part 1, section 1, Constitution of Arizona.
