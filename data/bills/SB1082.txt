(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 16-226, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE16-226.Nonpartisan elections; time of calling; definition
A.Nonpartisan elections shall be called no later
than one hundred twenty days before the date of holding the election.
B.For the purposes
of this chapter article,
"nonpartisan" means an election that is held by a special district
established pursuant to title 48 and that is not held concurrently with the
general election. END_STATUTE
