Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 41-1304.01, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE41-1304.01.Continuing code revision
A.The director of the Arizona legislative council
shall provide for:
1.The preparation and submission to the legislature
of a report as to defects in the constitution and laws of Arizona.
2.The drafting in the form of bills and resolutions
of proposed legislation to carry out the recommendations contained in the
report as to defects in the constitution and laws of Arizona.All
drafting in the form of bills and resolutions of proposed legislation to
correct defects in the constitution and laws of Arizona that were subject to a
successful initiative or
referendum shall be submitted to the legislature separately from all other
drafts to correct reported defects.
3.The arranging for publication of annotations of
decisions of the supreme court of Arizona.
4.The preparation, arranging and correlation for
publication, at the end of each legislative session, of the laws enacted during
the session.
5.The preparation, arranging and correlation for
publication of other laws enacted.
B.The director may prepare for submission to the
legislature a rewriting and revision, in simplified style, phraseology and
order, of any title, chapter, article or sections of the Arizona Revised
Statutes. END_STATUTE
