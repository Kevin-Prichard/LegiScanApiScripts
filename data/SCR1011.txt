A CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

recognizing Benson Primary School for outstanding
achievement.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Whereas, Benson
Primary School was designated as a "2016 Game Plan for Success Learning
Heroes Winner," an honor bestowed on only two schools in Arizona and ten
schools in the nation, and as a "2015 A+ School of Excellence" by the
Arizona Educational Foundation; and
Whereas, in 2013,
2014 and 2015, Benson Primary School was named as a "High Performing
Reward School" by the Arizona Department of Education; and
Whereas, the
United States Department of Education recognized Benson Primary School as a
"2014 Exemplary Achievement Gap Closing National Blue Ribbon School of
Excellence," an honor bestowed on only four schools in Arizona and 48
schools in the nation; and
Whereas, on
December 8, 2014, the Arizona State Board of Education recognized Benson
Primary School for its outstanding leadership, dedication and hard work; and
Whereas, Benson
Primary School has been designated as an "A" label school by the
Arizona Department of Education in 2011, 2012, 2013 and 2014 and was within the
number one ranked district in the State of Arizona in 2013 and was within the
top ten districts in the State of Arizona in 2014; and
Whereas, in 2013,
Benson Primary School was recognized as the number one traditional elementary
school in all of Cochise County and as an Arizona Department of Education Title
I Reward School; and
Whereas, in 2012,
2013 and 2014, the National Center for Educational Achievement, a department of
Advancing Toward College and Career Readiness, identified Benson Primary School
as a Higher Performing School.
Therefore
Be it resolved by the Senate of the State of Arizona, the House
of Representatives concurring:
That the Members of the Legislature recognize the
remarkable efforts and accomplishments of the faculty, staff and students of
the Benson Primary School.
