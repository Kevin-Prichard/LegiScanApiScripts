AN ACT

amending title 44,
chapter 3, article 1, Arizona Revised Statutes, by adding section 44-340;
relating to unclaimed property.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 44, chapter 3, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 44-340, to read:
START_STATUTE44-340.Auditor contingency contracts prohibited
The department may not
enter into a contract with an auditor for an audit relating to unclaimed
property pursuant to this chapter if the contract provides that the
department's payment to the auditor is contingent on the auditor recovering
unclaimed property. END_STATUTE
Sec.2.Applicability
Section 44-340, Arizona Revised Statutes, as added by this act,
applies only to contracts that are entered into on or after the effective date
of this act.
