Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 1, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-117, to read:
START_STATUTE15-117.Immunization rate; website posting
Each public school,
including each charter school, that maintains a website shall post on the
website the immunization rate of pupils who are enrolled at that school. END_STATUTE
