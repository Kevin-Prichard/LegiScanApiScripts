Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 23, chapter 2, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 23-205, to read:
START_STATUTE23-205.Wage disclosure; prohibitions
An employer may not:
1.Discharge,
formally discipline or otherwise discriminate against an employee because the
employee discloses the amount of the employee's wages, salary or paid benefits.
2.Require
an employee to sign a waiver or other document that prohibits the employee from
disclosing the employee's wage, salary or benefit information. END_STATUTE
