Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Laws 2013, chapter 255, section 28
is amended to read:
Sec.28.Revenue impact report
A.The joint legislative budget committee shall
prepare a report of the revenue impact analysis resulting from this act on or
before September 30, 2016. The analysis shall include an estimated impact on
revenues for this state and the counties,
cities and towns.
B.The joint legislative budget committee shall
provide copies of the report to the governor, president of the senate, speaker
of the house of representatives, governor's office of strategic planning and
budgeting and secretary of state.
