Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of water resources; Navajo
nation department of water resources; Kayenta dam; exemption
A.The sum of $1,500,000 is appropriated from the
state general fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of water
resources for distribution to the Navajo nation department of water resources
for payment of costs incurred by the Navajo nation department of water
resources in reconstructing Kayenta dam.
B.The appropriation made in subsection A of this
section is exempt from the provisions of section 35-190, Arizona Revised
Statutes, relating to lapsing of appropriations.
