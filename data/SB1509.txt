Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 36, chapter 29, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 36-2903.12, to read:
START_STATUTE36-2903.12.AHCCCS contractors; mobile dentistry; informed consent
A.A
contractor performing mobile dentistry services pursuant to title 32, chapter
11, article 8 shall obtain from the parent or guardian of the patient
appropriate informed consent, in writing or by verbal communication, that is
recorded by an electronic or digital device and that authorizes specific
treatment before it is performed. The signed consent form or verbal
communication shall be maintained as part of the patient's record as required
in section 321264.
B.The
signed consent form or verbal communication shall inform the parent or guardian
that the treatment of the minor by the mobile dental facility or portable
dental unit may affect future benefits the minor may receive under the system.
C.At the
conclusion of each patient's visit, the contractor shall provide the parent or
guardian of the patient with an information sheet that contains:
1.Pertinent
contact information as required by title 32, chapter 11, article 8.
2.The name
of the dentist or dental hygienist, or both, who provided services.
3.A
description of the treatment rendered, including billed service codes, fees
associated with treatment and tooth numbers if appropriate.
4.If
necessary, referral information to another dentist as required by title 32,
chapter 11, article 8.
D.If the
patient's parent or guardian has provided written consent to an institutional
facility to access the patient's dental health records, the contractor shall
provide the institution with a copy of the information sheet provided in
subsection C of this section. END_STATUTE
