Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 28, chapter 3, article 13,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 28-878, to read:
START_STATUTE28-878.Parking spaces for motor vehicles with purple heart
special plates
A.Each
state agency having jurisdiction over street parking or publicly owned and
operated parking facilities may provide specially designated and marked motor
vehicle parking spaces for the exclusive use of an operator of a motor vehicle
that displays a purple heart special plate that is issued pursuant to section
28-2474.
B.each
political subdivision of this state having jurisdiction over street parking or
publicly owned and operated parking facilities may provide, as determined by
the county board of supervisors or the governing body of a city or town,
specially designated and marked motor vehicle parking spaces for the exclusive
use of an operator of a motor vehicle that displays a purple heart special
plate that is issued pursuant to section 28-2474.
C.Each
parking space described in this section shall be prominently outlined with
paint and posted with a permanent sign that is located at least three feet but
not more than six feet above the grade, that is of a color and design approved
by the department and that bears the caption "reserved parking".
D.Except as
provided in subsection E of this section, a person may not stop, stand or park
a motor vehicle within any specially designated and marked parking space
provided pursuant to this section unless the motor vehicle displays a purple
heart special plate that is issued pursuant to section 28-2474.
E.A person
who is chauffeuring a person who is a purple heart medal recipient in a motor
vehicle without a purple heart special plate may park momentarily in a parking
space provided pursuant to this section for the purpose of loading or unloading
the person who is a purple heart medal recipient, and a complaint may not be
issued to the driver for the momentary parking.END_STATUTE
