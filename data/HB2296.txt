Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 16-922, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE16-922.Religious assembly, institution; charitable organization;
registration exemption
Notwithstanding any other law, this state and any agency or
political subdivision of this state shall not require a person or entity to register as a political committee
or file any reports pursuant
to this chapter if the person or entity
is a religious assembly or institution or
is any other entity with a charitable tax exemption under section 501(c)(3) of the internal revenue code or any
successor provision of federal tax law that does not spend a
substantial amount of time or assets, within the meaning of section 501(c)(3)
of the internal revenue code, on influencing any federal, state or local legislation,
referendum, initiative or constitutional amendment. END_STATUTE
