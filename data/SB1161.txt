Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; corporation commission; searchable
database; exemption
A.The sum of $2,000,000 is appropriated from the
public access fund established by section 10-122.01, Arizona Revised Statutes,
in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the corporation commission for a new searchable
database of business entity filings.
B.The appropriation made in subsection A of this
section is exempt from the provisions of section 35-190, Arizona Revised
Statutes, relating to lapsing of appropriations.
