Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 40-334, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE40-334.Discrimination between persons, localities or classes of
service as to rates, charges, service or facilities prohibited
A.A public service corporation shall not, as to
rates, charges, service, facilities or in any other respect, make or grant any
preference or advantage to any person or subject any person to any prejudice or
disadvantage.
B.No public service corporation shall establish or
maintain any unreasonable difference as to rates, charges, service, facilities
or in any other respect, either between localities or between classes of
service.
C.A water
system and a wastewater system may not consolidate unless the systems are
contiguous to each other.
C.D.The
commission may determine any question of fact arising under this section. END_STATUTE
