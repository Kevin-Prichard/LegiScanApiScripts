Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of education; K-3 reading
intervention
The sum of $40,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of education for distribution
to school districts and charter schools for K-3 reading
intervention.The department shall distribute the monies to school
districts and charter schools based on the number of third grade students in
each school district or charter school who receive a score of minimally
proficient or partially proficient on the English language arts portion of the
statewide assessment to measure academic standards.
