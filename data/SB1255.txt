(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3017.01,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3022.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3022.09.Vehicle emissions inspection program; termination July 1,
2022
A.The
vehicle emissions inspection program terminates on July 1, 2022.
B.Title 49,
chapter 3, article 5 is repealed on January 1, 2023.END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 41-2955,
subsection B, Arizona Revised Statutes, the legislature continues the vehicle
emissions inspection program to identify violations and compel compliance with
vehicle emissions requirements.
