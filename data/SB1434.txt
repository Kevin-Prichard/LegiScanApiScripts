Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 41, chapter 32, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3509, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3509.Consolidation and shared services; information technology
projects 
A.The
department shall IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITIES for information technology
consolidation and shared services, including consolidating servers and data
centers.
B.Before a
budget unit purchases or contracts for an information technology project that
exceeds fifty thousand dollars, the budget unit shall submit the project to the
department for evaluation. The department shall evaluate the project to
determine if the project is suitable for consolidation or shared services with
another budget unit. If the department deems the project to be suitable for
consolidation or shared services, the department shall make a recommendation to
the chief information officers of the three largest budget units.If
at least two of the three chief information officers agree that the project is
suitable for consolidation or shared services, the department shall notify the
appropriate budget units of the determination. if it is determined that the
project is not suitable for consolidation or shared services, the department
shall notify the budget unit that submitted the project of the determination.
C.If two or
more budget units are notified that the information technology project that is
submitted for evaluation pursuant to subsection B of this section has been
deemed suitable for consolidation or shared services, the budget units shall
proceed with a consolidated purchase or contract. END_STATUTE
