Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Arizona minority veterans memorial; procedures; delayed
repeal
A.Pursuant to section 411363, subsection A,
Arizona Revised Statutes, this act authorizes the department of administration
to provide for the placement in Wesley Bolin plaza of a memorial dedicated to
the commemoration of Arizona minority veterans.
B.The procedures prescribed in section 411363,
Arizona Revised Statutes, apply to the establishment of the memorial.
C.Public monies are not authorized for the costs of
the memorial. All fundraising and contracts for artistic design and
construction of the memorial are the sole responsibility of the proponents.
This state may not facilitate fundraising or establish a state fund for
the deposit of the monies.
D.This section is repealed from and after September
30, 2019.
