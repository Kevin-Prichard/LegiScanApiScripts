Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.22,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Arizona department of homeland security; termination July
1, 2024
A.The
Arizona department of homeland security terminates on July 1, 2024.
B.Title 41,
chapter 41 is repealed on January 1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 412955, subsection B, Arizona
Revised Statutes, the legislature continues the Arizona department of homeland
security to enhance the ability of this state to prevent and respond to acts of
terrorism and other critical hazards in this state.
Sec.4.Retroactivity 
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
