Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal; hospitalization and medical care contribution; no
refund obligation
A.Laws 2015, chapter 14, section 13 is repealed.
B.This state has no obligation to refund monies
paid pursuant to Laws 2015, chapter 14, section 13.
