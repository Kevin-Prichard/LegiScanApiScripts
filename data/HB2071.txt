Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 35-188, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE35-188.Allowance of setoff against state
In all actions brought on behalf of the state, no debt shall be
allowed against the state as a setoff
setoff unless it has been
exhibited to the director of the department of administration and by him
allowed or disallowed. A debt may be allowed as a setoff setoff when it is proved to the satisfaction
of the court that the defendant, at the time of trial, is in possession of
vouchers which that he could not produce to
the department of administration, or that he was prevented from exhibiting the
claim to the department of administration by absence from the state, sickness
or unavoidable accident. END_STATUTE
