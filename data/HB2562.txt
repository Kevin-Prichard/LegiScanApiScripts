AN ACT

151805, Arizona Revised Statutes; relating to classification of students
for tuition purposes.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 15-1805, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE15-1805.Student status guidelines
A.The Arizona board of regents shall adopt
guidelines applicable to all institutions under their its
jurisdiction that will ensure uniform criteria to aid the
institutions in determining the tuition status of any student and that will
establish uniform procedures for review of that status.
B.Community college districts shall adopt policies
applicable to all institutions under their jurisdiction that will ensure
uniform criteria to aid the institutions in determining the tuition status of
any student and that will establish uniform procedures for review of that
status. END_STATUTE
