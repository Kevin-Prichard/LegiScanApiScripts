Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 7, article 5,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-782.01, to read:
START_STATUTE15-782.01.Instructors; specialized teaching certificate;
qualifications; rules
A.NOTWITHSTANDING
ANY OTHER LAW, The state board of education shall issue a specialized teaching
certificate in career and technical EDUCATION to A PERSON WHO PROVIDES
INSTRUCTION IN A CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION COURSE OR PROGRAM THAT IS
OFFERED BY A SCHOOL DISTRICT OR JOINT TECHNICAL EDUCATION DISTRICT IF THE
PERSON DOES ALL OF THE FOLLOWING:
1.Demonstrates
expertise in the subject matter in which the person will provide instruction.
2.DEMONSTRATES
CURRENT OR PRIOR WORK EXPERIENCE IN THE SUBJECT MATTER IN WHICH THE PERSON WILL
PROVIDE INSTRUCTION.
3.Has
either a baccalaureate degree, a master's degree or a doctorate degree in an
academic subject that is specific to the subject matter in which the person
will provide instruction.
4.COMPLIES
WITH THE FINGERPRINTING AND BACKGROUND CHECK REQUIREMENTS PRESCRIBED IN SECTION
15-512.
5.MEETS THE
REQUIREMENTS FOR A HIGHLY QUALIFIED TEACHER UNDER FEDERAL LAW.
B.The state
board of education shall adopt rules to carry out the purposes of this
section.END_STATUTE
