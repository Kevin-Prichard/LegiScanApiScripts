(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 11, chapter 2, article 4,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 11-269.15, to read:
START_STATUTE11-269.15.Acquisition of property for wireless communication
facilities
Notwithstanding section
11201, subsection A, paragraph 2, A county may purchase, own, lease or
hold real property outside of its territorial limits for the purpose of
developing, operating and maintaining wireless communication facilities that
are used for county government purposes.END_STATUTE
