Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Supplemental appropriation; department of health services;
training materials; proactive prevention education; exemption
A.In addition to the appropriation made by Laws
2015, chapter 8, section 50, the sum of $1,000,000 is appropriated from the
state general fund in fiscal year 2015-2016 to the department of health
services to provide grants on a competitive basis for the development of
training materials for a proactive prevention education program for middle and
high school students in this state. The program must:
1.Promote positive life choices by educating middle
and high school students about the harms and consequences of destructive
behaviors in order to reduce the motivation to abuse substances and be involved
in harmful social environments.
2.Include a proactive prevention component focused
on substance abuse, violence and other risky behaviors.
3.Include an educational component focused on
mental health.
B.The appropriation made in subsection A of this
section is exempt from the provisions of section 35-190, Arizona Revised
Statutes, relating to lapsing of appropriations.
