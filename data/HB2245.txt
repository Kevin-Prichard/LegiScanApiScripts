Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 1, article 5, Arizona
Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-158, to read:
START_STATUTE15-158.Cellular telephone towers on school property; worker
documentation and sign in; civil penalty
A.If a
cellular telephone tower is located on an easement that is granted on property
owned or leased by a public school, any person who accesses the easement for
the purpose of maintaining, inspecting or servicing that cellular telephone
tower shall sign in at the school office and provide governmentissued
identification to school office personnel before accessing the easement.
B.At the
request of the principal or head teacher of a school at which an alleged
violation of subsection A of this section occurred, The attorney general or the
county attorney for the county in which the alleged violation occurred may
serve on the employer of a person who is determined to be out of compliance
with this section an order requiring compliance with this section and may
assess a civil penalty of not more than ten thousand dollars for each violation
of subsection A of this section. The employer of the person is responsible for
paying the civil penalty. All civil penalties collected pursuant to this
subsection shall be transferred to the state treasurer for deposit, pursuant to
sections 35-146 and 35-147, in the state general fund. END_STATUTE
