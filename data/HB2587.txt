Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of transportation; Hopi route 60
construction
The sum of $3,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of transportation for
distribution to the Navajo nation for construction costs associated with the improvement
of Hopi route 60.
