AN ACT

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 12, chapter 8, article 14,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 12-1364, to read:
START_STATUTE12-1364.Dwelling action; attorney fees, expert witness fees and
taxable costs
In any contested
dwelling action, the court may award the successful party reasonable attorney
fees, reasonable expert witness fees and taxable costs.If the
judgment finally obtained is less than or less favorable than the repairs or
replacements and offers made by the seller pursuant to section 121363,
the seller is deemed to be the successful party. If the judgment finally
obtained is more favorable than the repairs or replacements and offers made by
the seller pursuant to section 121363, the purchaser is deemed to be the
successful party.This section does not alter, prohibit or restrict
present or future contracts or statutes that may provide for attorney fees. END_STATUTE
