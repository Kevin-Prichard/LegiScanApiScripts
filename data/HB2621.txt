Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 41, chapter 3, article 1.1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-511.24, to read:
START_STATUTE41-511.24.Veterans memorial state park; fund
A.The
Arizona state parks board shall:
1.Establish
the veterans memorial state park subject to the purchase of the veterans
memorial state park site pursuant to title 37, chapter 2, article 3.
2.Seek to
minimize the use of state general fund monies for the purchase, maintenance and
preservation of the veterans memorial state park.
3.Designate
a nonprofit organization that is exempt from taxation under section 501(c)(3) of the internal revenue code and that
has a mission to preserve, promote and enhance state parks in this state as the
fiscal agent to receive and administer on behalf of the Arizona state parks
board any monies that are paid into the veterans memorial fund established by
subsection c of this section and that are dedicated to the development of the
veterans memorial state park. 
4.Plan for
the maintenance and preservation of the veterans memorial state park.
B.The
Arizona state parks board may:
1.Work with
interested parties to develop the veterans memorial state park.
2.Accept
private monetary donations or public monies for deposit in the veterans
memorial fund established by subsection C of this section.
3.Enter into
an intergovernmental agreement pursuant to title 11, chapter 7, article 3 with
the county in which the park is located for the maintenance and preservation of
the veterans memorial state park.
C.The
veterans memorial fund is established consisting of legislative appropriations
and donations to the fund.The Arizona state parks board shall
administer the fund.On notice from the Arizona state parks board,
the state treasurer shall invest and divest monies in the fund as provided by
section 35-313, and monies earned from investment shall be credited to the
fund.The monies in the fund are exempt from section 35-190 relating
to lapsing of appropriations.The monies in the fund and any
additional donations to the fund shall be used for the purpose of purchasing
land for and maintaining and preserving the veterans memorial state park.END_STATUTE
Sec.2.Appropriation; Arizona state parks board
The sum of $800,000 is appropriated from the state general fund
in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the Arizona state parks board for the purposes of
this act.
