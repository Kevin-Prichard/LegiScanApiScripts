AN ACT

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 15-161, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE15-161.State control over private schools
Nothing in this title shall be construed to provide the state
board of education or the governing boards of school districts with control or supervision over private schools.
