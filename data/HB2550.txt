(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 41, chapter 32, article 1,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3509, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3509.Software development; definition
A.A budget
unit, or a private entity with funding, specifications and oversight from a budget
unit, may produce government off-the-shelf software and provide the government
off-the-shelf software to other budget units at no cost or with no warranty.
B.For the
purposes of this section, "government off-the-shelf software" means
software that is developed for a budget unit for one purpose and that is later
reused by another budget unit or integrated into a new government system or
application. END_STATUTE
