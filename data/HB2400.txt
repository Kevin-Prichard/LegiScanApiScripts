Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; Arizona exposition and state fair board;
preservation and renovation of historic buildings
The sum of $1,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the Arizona exposition and state fair board
for the preservation and renovation of historic buildings under the control of
the board.
