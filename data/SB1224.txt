Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 42-3307, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE42-3307.Preemption by state
This state preempts the area of Indian reservation tobacco
taxation.A county, city,
or town or any other
political subdivision of this state shall not levy such a tax. Nothing in this
article precludes an Indian tribe from imposing its own tobacco tax or a
similar levy.END_STATUTE
