Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 28-622.01, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE28-622.01.Unlawful flight from pursuing law enforcement vehicle;
violation; classification
A driver of a motor vehicle who wilfully flees or attempts to
elude a pursuing official law enforcement vehicle that is being operated in the
manner described in section 28624, subsection C is guilty of a class 5
felony.The law enforcement
vehicle shall be appropriately marked to show that it is an official law
enforcement vehicle. END_STATUTE
