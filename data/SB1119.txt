Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.11,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.11, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.11.Board of technical registration; termination July 1, 2024
A.The board
of technical registration terminates on July 1, 2024.
B.Title 32,
chapter 1 is repealed on January 1, 2025.END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 41-2955, subsection B, Arizona Revised
Statutes, the legislature continues the board of technical registration to
protect the public safety and welfare by regulating architects, assayers,
engineers, geologists, home inspectors, landscape architects and land
surveyors.
Sec.4.Retroactivity
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
