Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 28, chapter 20, article 5,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 28-7060, to read:
START_STATUTE28-7060.Human trafficking hotline signs
In each roadside rest
area or roadside park, the department shall place in a prominent location a
sign that clearly states the telephone number for a national toll-free hotline
for human trafficking. The sign shall inform the public that the telephone
number is to help human trafficking victims get help and stay safe. END_STATUTE
