(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 5-515.02, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE5-515.02.Purchase of lottery tickets or shares by persons under
twentyone years of age; penalty
A.Until June 1, 2003, it is unlawful for a person under
eighteen years of age to purchase a lottery ticket or share. Beginning on June
1, 2003, It is unlawful for a person under twentyone years
of age to purchase a lottery ticket or share.
B.Until June 1, 2003, a person under eighteen
years of age who knowingly purchases a lottery ticket or share is guilty of a
petty offense.Beginning on June 1, 2003, it is unlawful for a
person under twentyone years of age to purchase a lottery ticket or
share. 
b.a knowing violation of subsection a is a petty
offense.END_STATUTE
