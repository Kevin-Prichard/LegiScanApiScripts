Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 15-1622, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE15-1622.Officers; organization; quorum
A.The board shall select from its membership a
president, secretary,
and treasurer and such
other officers it deems necessary.The same person shall not hold
the offices of secretary and treasurer.The board may require the
treasurer to give an additional
bond.
B.A majority of the membership of the board shall
constitute a quorum for the transaction
of business at any meeting regularly called, but a number less than a quorum
may adjourn from time to time. END_STATUTE
