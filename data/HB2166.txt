AN ACT

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 33-1241, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE33-1241.Organization of unit owners' association
A unit owners' association shall be organized no later than the
date the first unit in the condominium is initially conveyed.The membership
of the association at all times shall consist exclusively of all the unit
owners or, following termination of the condominium, of all former unit owners
entitled to distributions of proceeds under section 331228, or their
heirs, successors or assigns.The association shall be organized as
a profit or nonprofit corporation or as an unincorporated association.END_STATUTE
