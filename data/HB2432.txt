Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 41-3501, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE41-3501.Definitions
In this chapter, unless the context otherwise requires:
1."Budget unit" means a department,
commission, board, institution or other agency of the state receiving, expending
or disbursing state funds or incurring obligations of the state including the
Arizona board of regents but excluding the universities under the jurisdiction
of the Arizona board of regents, the community college districts and the
legislative or judicial branches.
2."Committee" means the information
technology authorization committee.
3."Department" means the department of
administration.
4."Director" means the director of the
department.
5."Disaster recovery" means the measures
required to mitigate the loss of information technology capability.
6."Information technology" means all
computerized and auxiliary automated information processing, telecommunications, interoperable radio communications systems
and related technology, including hardware, software, vendor support and
related services, equipment and projects. END_STATUTE
