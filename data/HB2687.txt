Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of education; college entrance
examinations 
The sum of $3,100,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of education for distribution
to school districts and charter schools to fund the costs for each pupil in
grade eleven to take a nationally recognized college entrance examination.
