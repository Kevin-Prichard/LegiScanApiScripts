Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of veterans' services; veterans'
home
The sum of $10,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of veterans' services for
distribution to the city of Flagstaff for construction of a veterans' home in
Flagstaff.
