Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; department of economic security; Navajo
nation supreme court complex
The sum of $7,500,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the department of economic security for distribution
to the Navajo nation for the construction of a supreme court complex.
