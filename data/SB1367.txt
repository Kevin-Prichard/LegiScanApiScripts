Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Assyrian genocide commemoration monument; procedures;
delayed repeal
A.Pursuant to section 41-1363, subsection A,
Arizona Revised Statutes, this act authorizes the department of administration
to provide for the placement in Wesley Bolin plaza of a monument dedicated to
the commemoration of Assyrian Christians who were killed as a result of the
Assyrian genocide.
B.The procedures prescribed in section 41-1363,
Arizona Revised Statutes, apply to the establishment of the monument.
C.Public monies are not authorized for the costs of
the monument. All fund-raising and contracts for artistic design and
construction of the monument are the sole responsibility of the proponents.
This state may not facilitate fund-raising or establish a state fund for the
deposit of the monies.
D.This section is repealed from and after September
30, 2019.
