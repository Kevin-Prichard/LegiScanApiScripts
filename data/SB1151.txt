(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.19,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Arizona state retirement system; termination July 1, 2024
A.The Arizona state retirement system
board and director terminate on July 1, 2024.
B.Title
38, chapter 5, articles 2 and 2.1 are repealed on January 1, 2025.END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 41-2955, subsection B, Arizona Revised
Statutes, the legislature continues the Arizona state retirement system board
and director in order to manage and invest employee and employer contributions
and assets and distribute retirement and long-term disability benefits to
retired members of the system and plan.
Sec.4.Retroactivity
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
