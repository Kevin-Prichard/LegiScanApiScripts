Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Appropriation; assistance for education fund; solutions
teams 
The sum of $3,000,000 is appropriated from the state general
fund in fiscal year 2016-2017 to the assistance for education fund established
by section 15-973.01, Arizona Revised Statutes, to fund solutions teams
assigned to schools pursuant to section 15-241, Arizona Revised Statutes.
