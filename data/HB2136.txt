Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 35-741, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE35-741.Exemption from taxation
The corporation and its income and all bonds issued by it and
the income therefrom shall be exempt from all taxation in this state, except
that property of the corporation shall be subject to all applicable ad valorem
taxes.If such property is leased, such ad valorem taxes shall be
charged to the lessee as fully as if the lessee were the owner of such leased
property except that no ad valorem tax shall be imposed upon on
any property of the corporation which
that is leased to a
person qualified for exemption from such taxes by article 9
IX, section 2, Constitution of Arizona, and no charge for such a tax shall be made
to any such lessee. END_STATUTE
