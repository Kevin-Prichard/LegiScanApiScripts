(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.31,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.09, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.09.Arizona commission of Indian affairs; termination July 1,
2024
A.The
Arizona commission of Indian affairs terminates on July 1, 2024.
B.Title 41,
chapter 3, article 4 is repealed on January 1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 412955, subsection B, Arizona
Revised Statutes, the legislature continues the Arizona commission of Indian
affairs in order to assist and support Indians and tribal councils in this
state and to enhance governmenttogovernment relations between the
twentyone Arizona Indian tribes or nations and this state.
Sec.4.Retroactivity
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
