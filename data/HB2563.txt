(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 42-6006, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE42-6006.Municipal elections on tax issues
A city or town may submit any issue relating to a transaction
privilege tax, sales, use,
franchise or other similar tax or fee, however denominated, to the qualified
electors of the city or town at any regular or special municipal election, and
may spend public monies of the city or town to cover the expenses of the
election on that issue. END_STATUTE
