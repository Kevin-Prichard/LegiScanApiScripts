AN ACT

(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 14, article 6,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-1865, to read:
START_STATUTE15-1865.Free speech zone; prohibition; conversion
A.A
community college or university may not designate any area on campus as a free
speech zone.
B.Any free
speech zone that exists on the campus of a community college or university on
the effective date of this section shall be converted to a monument or
memorial. END_STATUTE
