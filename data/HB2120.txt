Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 9-237, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE9-237.Appointive officers
In addition to the common council, the officers of every town
shall be a town clerk, town marshal,
and town engineer and
other officers deemed necessary by the common council, who shall be appointed
as provided by ordinance of the town. END_STATUTE
