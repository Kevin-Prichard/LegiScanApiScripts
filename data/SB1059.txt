(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Repeal
Section 41-3016.14,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is repealed.
Sec.2.Title 41, chapter 27, article 2,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 41-3024.16, to read:
START_STATUTE41-3024.16.Oil and gas conservation commission; termination July 1,
2024
A.The oil
and gas conservation commission terminates on July 1, 2024.
B.Title 27,
chapter 4, article 1 is repealed on January 1, 2025. END_STATUTE
Sec.3.Purpose
Pursuant to section 412955, subsection B, Arizona
Revised Statutes, the legislature continues the oil and gas conservation
commission to regulate the drilling for and production of oil, gas, helium and
geothermal resources, promote the conservation of these resources, protect
ownership rights of these resources, safeguard the public health and otherwise
effect the public policy of this state pursuant to section 27502,
Arizona Revised Statutes.
Sec.4.Retroactivity
Sections 1 and 2 of this act are effective retroactively to
July 1, 2016.
