Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 46-201, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE46-201.Application for assistance; notice of penalties
A.Application for any form of assistance or service
under this title shall be made to a department in this state as specified by
the program.The application shall be in writing or reduced to
writing upon on forms prescribed by the state department
and shall be verified by the oath of
include a statement subscribed by the
applicant and certifying that the contents of the
application are true under penalty of perjury. The application shall bear
the applicant's witnessed
signature, which may be electronic.The
application shall contain a statement of the amount of property both personal
and real in which the applicant has an interest and of all income which that the applicant has at the time of filing
the application, and a statement of any property assigned or transferred by the
applicant within one year immediately prior
to before
filing the application for assistance, and any further information prescribed
by the state department.
B.The department shall use application forms which that contain appropriate notice of the
penalties for fraud and shall deliver to each recipient prior to
before the first payment of assistance or delivery of service and
each redetermination thereafter a notice explaining what changes in
circumstances require written notification to the department pursuant to
section 46213, subsection A. END_STATUTE
