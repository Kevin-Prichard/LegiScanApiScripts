AN ACT

Amending section 11-864,
Arizona Revised Statutes; relating to county building codes.


(TEXT OF BILL BEGINS ON NEXT PAGE)





Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Section 11-864, Arizona Revised
Statutes, is amended to read:
START_STATUTE11-864.Publication of ordinance adopting code
Any code authorized by this article may be enacted without
setting forth in full such the provisions, but the
adopting ordinance shall be published in full.At least three paper copies or one paper copy and one electronic copy maintained in
compliance with section 447041 of the code shall be filed
in the office of the clerk of the board of supervisors or the county planning and zoning department and
kept available for public use and inspection.A code enacted by
reference may be amended in the same manner. END_STATUTE
