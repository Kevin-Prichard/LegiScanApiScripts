Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 15, chapter 12, article 4,
Arizona Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 15-1475, to read:
START_STATUTE15-1475.Athletic facilities; nonprofit community groups;
prohibition of fees
A community college
district may not charge a fee to a nonprofit community group that is exempt
from federal income taxes under section 501(c)(3) of the internal revenue code and that uses athletic
facilities on a community college campus unless the use of the athletic
facilities by the nonprofit community group directly results in a significant
additional expense to the community college district. A community college
district may require a nonprofit community group to provide proof of insurance
and to fully indemnify and hold harmless the community college district against
any liability for loss or damages incurred at the athletic facilities. END_STATUTE
