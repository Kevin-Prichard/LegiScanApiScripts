Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Arizona:
Section1.Title 13, chapter 23, Arizona
Revised Statutes, is amended by adding section 13-2324, to read:
START_STATUTE13-2324.Continuous smuggling; classification
A.A person
who over a period of ten days or more in duration engages in two or more acts
in violation of section 132319 is guilty of continuous smuggling.
B.A person
may not be charged with more than one violation of this section if all of the
conduct that constitutes an offense under section 132319 is alleged to
have been committed against the same victim.
C.Continuous
smuggling is a class 2 felony and the person is not eligible for suspension of
sentence, probation, pardon or release from confinement on any other basis
except pursuant to section 31233, subsection A or B until the sentence
imposed by the court is served, the person is eligible for release pursuant to
section 411604.07 or the sentence is commuted.END_STATUTE
